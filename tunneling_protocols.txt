Alternative Layer 2 Tunneling Protocols

- L2TP/IPsec: Industry standard, supported natively by most OS kernels.
- SoftEther VPN: Multi-protocol VPN software excelling at virtual Ethernet bridging.
- ZeroTier: Software-defined networking solution acting as a virtual global Ethernet switch.
- OpenVPN (TAP Mode): Classic Ethernet bridging solution (TAP mode instead of TUN).
- VXLAN: Enterprise standard for L2-over-L3 tunneling.
- Tailscale: L3 VPN but can handle some subnet routing.
